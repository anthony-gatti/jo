5 mins with... sophie turner

  • How did you start? I was discovered through my school. I mean I wasn't necessarily discovered; I just auditioned for Game of Thrones. They actually liked me so they called me back four or five times. It was the best moment of my life so far because I've wanted to be an actress since I was three so it was like… it finally happened!

    What's it like to be the star of the Karen Millen AW14 campaign? Everyone knows Karen Millen. It's incredible. I'm so excited to be a part of it.

    How do you feel about being shot by Glen? It's really exciting to be shot by Glen because he's done such good campaigns. He's done Yves Saint Laurent, Calvin Klein. He's a big inspiration for a lot of photographers out there so it's quite exciting to work with such talent.

  • What's your favourite piece from the collection? The faux fur black coat. It's incredible. It's massive!

    How would you describe yourpersonal style? My personal style is a bit boring. It's just jeans and a t-shirt and that will do me nicely. And trainers! It's all about the comfort.

    What's next? I want to do as much as possible in as little time as possible. So more modelling, more acting. No singing!
